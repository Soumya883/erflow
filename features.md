# ERFlow Application Features

Based on the structure of the `erflow` application and the requirements of a complete office ERP system, here is a comprehensive list of its features and modules. This system is designed to be a robust Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Human Resources Management System (HRMS) combined with team collaboration tools.

## 1. Core HR & Employee Management
- **Employee Directory**: View a complete list of employees and their details.
- **Add/Edit Employee**: Manage employee records, onboarding, and profile updates.
- **Department Management**: Organize employees into different departments for better structural overview.
- **Recruitment/ATS**: Job postings, applicant tracking, and interview scheduling.
- **Performance Management**: Appraisals, KPIs, 360° feedback, and goal setting.
- **Payroll & Compliance Documents**: Offer letters, contracts, and PF/ESI (if region-specific).
- **Employee Self-Service Portal**: Access to payslips, tax forms, and personal documents.
- **Training & Learning Management (LMS)**: Onboarding courses, certifications, and skill tracking.
- **Exit Management**: Resignation workflow, exit interviews, and clearance tracking.
- **Feedback/Survey Tools**: Employee engagement surveys.

## 2. Attendance & Leave Management
- **Attendance Tracking**: Track and manage employee attendance.
- **Edit Attendance**: Allows authorized users to correct or modify attendance records.
- **Export Attendance**: Generate and export attendance reports (e.g., as CSV or Excel files).
- **Leave Requests**: Employees can request time off using dedicated modals, with workflows for approval.
- **Biometric/GPS Attendance**: For field employees or multi-location offices.

## 3. Finance & Accounting
- **Payroll Management**: Salary processing, tax deductions, and payslip generation.
- **Invoicing & Billing**: Create and send invoices, and handle recurring billing.
- **Expense Management**: Expense claims, reimbursement workflows, and receipt uploads.
- **Budgeting**: Department and project budget allocation and tracking.
- **Accounts Payable/Receivable**: Vendor payments and client dues tracking.
- **Tax Compliance**: GST/VAT handling and automated tax reports.

## 4. Task & Project Management
- **Projects**: Manage various internal or external projects.
- **Tasks**: Create, assign, and track individual tasks.
- **Kanban Boards**: Visual task management using Kanban-style drag-and-drop boards to track progress.
- **Gantt Charts**: Timeline view for project planning and dependency tracking.
- **Time Tracking/Timesheets**: Log hours spent against specific tasks and projects.
- **Resource Allocation**: Workload balancing across employees.
- **Milestone Tracking**: Define and track major project milestones.

## 5. Communication & Collaboration
- **Internal Chat**: Real-time messaging platform for employees to communicate directly within the app.
- **Announcements**: Broadcast important company-wide news, updates, or alerts.
- **Calendar**: Shared calendar for scheduling, tracking events, meetings, and important deadlines.
- **Video Conferencing Integration**: Zoom, Google Meet embed, or built-in video calls.
- **Email Integration**: Sync company email within the application.
- **Notification System**: Push, email, and in-app alerts for approvals and deadlines.
- **Helpdesk/Ticketing**: Internal IT or HR support ticketing system.
- **Meeting Room Booking System**: Reserve office spaces and meeting rooms.

## 6. Document Management
- **Document Hub**: Centralized repository for company files.
- **Create/Upload Documents**: Easily add new documents to the system with structured organization.

## 7. Asset & Inventory
- **Asset Management**: Track company assets (laptops, furniture, equipment) assigned to employees.
- **Inventory Management**: Stock levels, procurement, and warehouse tracking.
- **Vendor/Supplier Management**: Vendor database and purchase order management.

## 8. CRM & Client Management
- **Lead & Client Management**: Track sales pipeline, interactions, and customer relationships.
- **Contract Management**: Client and vendor contracts, renewals, and e-signatures.

## 9. Analytics & Reporting
- **Dashboards**: High-level overview of key metrics, tasks, and system activities.
- **Analytics**: Data visualizations regarding employee performance, attendance, and project progress.
- **Custom Report Builder**: Generate ad-hoc reports based on varied data points.
- **Predictive Analytics**: Attrition risk analysis and project delay forecasting.
- **Export to Multiple Formats**: Export reports and data to PDF, Excel, and PowerPoint.

## 10. Compliance & Security
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Granular permissions tailored by role or department.
- **Audit Logs**: Track user activity, changes made, and timestamps.
- **Data Backup & Recovery**: Automated and manual data backup tools.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Enhanced login security.
- **GDPR/Data Privacy Compliance**: Tools and workflows to ensure data privacy regulations are met.

## 11. System, Mobile & Integration
- **Settings & Theming**: Custom themes (light/dark modes) and application configuration.
- **Responsive Layout**: Sidebar navigation and modular layouts for seamless use across devices.
- **Mobile App**: iOS and Android companion applications.
- **API/Third-party Integrations**: Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, accounting software (Tally, QuickBooks).
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)**: Simplified and secure login across multiple services.
- **Multi-language Support**: For localized application use.
- **Multi-currency Support**: Essential for global offices and financial tracking.
- **Offline Mode/Sync**: Functionality and data sync capabilities when internet connectivity is poor or absent.
